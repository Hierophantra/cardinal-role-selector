# Milestone v1.3: Monday Prep Redesign

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2026-04-13
**Phases:** 12-13
**Total Plans:** 3

## Overview

Monday Prep meeting gets its own intention-focused structure, distinct from Friday Review's KPI-focused agenda. Clear the Air stop added to both meeting types as the first stop to surface interpersonal/emotional context before tactical discussion. Friday Review expands from 12 to 13 stops; Monday Prep runs on 5 planning-focused stops (Clear the Air, Week Preview, Priorities & Focus, Risks & Blockers, Commitments & Action Items).
